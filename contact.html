Quick Answers

Frequently asked questions

Who can join Kipaji Spark programs?

Our programs are designed for children and youth of various ages. Contact us to learn which program best fits your child or group.

Can schools or organizations collaborate with Kipaji Spark?

Absolutely. We actively partner with schools, community organizations, and other groups to expand access to talent development opportunities.

How do I ask about an upcoming event?

Use our contact form or email us directly. Select "Events" as your inquiry type and we'll get back to you with details.

Can I volunteer or support a program?

Yes! We welcome volunteers, mentors, and supporters. Reach out through the form or email to start the conversation.
