Events & Experiences

Events that celebrate talent, build confidence, and bring young people into inspiring spaces

Kipaji Spark organizes showcases, workshops, competitions, mentorship experiences, outreach gatherings, and youth-centered events that help children and young people grow in creativity, confidence, leadership, and visibility.

Join an Event Partner With Us
Why We Host Events

Creating spaces where talent is seen, nurtured, and celebrated

Kipaji Spark events are not just gatherings — they are intentional experiences that help children and youth express themselves publicly, gain confidence, connect with mentors and peers, learn through workshops and performance opportunities, and access supportive community spaces.

Every event is designed to turn hidden talent into visible growth and to give young people the stage, the skills, and the encouragement they deserve.

What our events create

  • Confidence through performance
  • Exposure and visibility
  • Mentorship and inspiration
  • Creative expression
  • Community connection

Signature Experiences

Events that define the Kipaji Spark journey

Flagship Event

Arusha's Got Talent

Our most celebrated event experience — a talent competition and showcase that gives children and youth a stage to perform, be seen, and gain confidence in front of their community. This event brings together schools, families, mentors, and partners to celebrate young talent in a powerful way.

  • Live performances and talent showcases
  • School and community participation
  • Recognition, visibility, and celebration
  • Confidence-building through public performance
Workshop Series

Talent Workshops & Mentorship Sessions

Interactive, hands-on event experiences where children and youth learn directly from mentors, coaches, and creative professionals. These sessions are designed to build skills, spark creativity, and provide guided practice in a supportive environment.

  • Skill-building through guided practice
  • Direct access to mentors and coaches
  • Creative exploration in a safe space
  • Peer connection and collaboration
Community Experience

Community Outreach & Youth Gatherings

Events that bring the Kipaji Spark experience beyond our walls — into schools, neighborhoods, and community spaces. These gatherings are about expanding access, building supportive ecosystems, and ensuring that more children and youth can experience the joy and growth of talent-centered events.

  • Outreach to schools and communities
  • Family and stakeholder engagement
  • Accessible creative and performance experiences
  • Building local support networks for youth talent
